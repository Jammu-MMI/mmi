About Us

Modern Montessori International – Jammu

Modern Montessori International (MMI) is an internationally recognised Montessori organisation headquartered in London, with centres across India and beyond. MMI Jammu is proud to bring this world-class Montessori education to the children of Jammu & Kashmir.

Our school offers a rich, child-centred learning environment based on the authentic Montessori philosophy — where every child is seen as a unique individual, capable of driving their own learning when given the right environment, materials and guidance.

  • Internationally Accredited Montessori Programme
  • Trained & Certified Montessori Educators
  • Authentic Montessori Materials from Birth to 6 Years
  • Safe, Nurturing & Child-Friendly Environment
  • MMI Uniform & Structured Daily Routine
"Giving the child a Montessori beginning is the best gift parents can give their child."
Why Montessori?

The Montessori Difference

The Montessori Method, developed by Dr. Maria Montessori, recognises that children pass through sensitive periods of development during which they are uniquely receptive to specific types of learning.

Through specially designed multisensory learning materials, children at MMI Jammu engage all their senses to develop a deep, holistic understanding of the world around them — building confidence, independence and a lifelong love of learning.

  • Child-centred, self-directed learning
  • Mixed-age groups (2½ – 6 years)
  • Hands-on Montessori materials
  • Specially trained Montessori educators

Dr. Maria Montessori
Founder of the Montessori Method

Dr. Maria Montessori
"We cannot create observers by saying 'observe', but by giving them the power and the means for this observation and these means are procured through education of the senses."

The Montessori philosophy places the child at the centre of their own learning journey. Every material, every activity and every interaction at MMI Jammu is designed with this profound principle in mind — to educate the whole child.
